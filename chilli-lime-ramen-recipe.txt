Description: recipe for chilli lime ramen.

Ingredients
Qty	Description
1	Maruchan ramen package (chicken, beef, or shirmp)
2 Cup	boiling water
2 	egg
1 tbs	lime juice
2 tbs	sriracha

Instructions
- bring water to boil
- open maruchan ramen package and place noodle brick into boiling water
- once noodles have started to separate open flavor packets and mix into water
- add lime juice
- crack both eggs into soup and ladel soup broth over eggs to poach
- add sriracha hot sauce to soup
- transfer to bowl and garnish with konbu (seaweed) and/or bean sprouts
- enjoy
